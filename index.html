About Us

Echelon Dev Society is the official hackathon and developer community of CDGI college. We are a group of passionate innovators, coders, and designers who love building impactful tech solutions. Our mission is to inspire, collaborate, and create through events like hackathons, coding challenges, and open-source projects.
